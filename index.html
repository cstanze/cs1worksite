Programming Challenge 3: Shapes World Problem Solving

Four separate Java programs for this assignment, all in the context of the NPW, we will want to reflect more upon the creation and use of objects in these programs than on the structure of the programs. /The use of arithmetic operators to directly compute areas or perimeters of shapes, diagonals of squares or rectangles, diameters of circles, is strictly prohibited. Thus, you must create and use shapes in order to compute such values.
